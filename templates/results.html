How to Interpret
  • R² — proportion of variance in Y explained by the model. Values closer to 1 are better.
  • RMSE — average prediction error in the same units as Y. Lower is better.
  • Train vs Test gap — a large difference indicates overfitting; consider lowering the polynomial degree.
  • Residuals — ideally scattered randomly around zero. Patterns suggest the model misses structure in the data.
  • Coefficients — for degree-1 models, each unit increase in a predictor changes Y by its coefficient (all else equal).
